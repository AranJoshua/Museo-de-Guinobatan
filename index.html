Museo de Guinobatan

Step inside and explore the spirit of our Municipality!

Simeon Ola was a brave Filipino leader who fought hard against Spanish rule in the Bicol region. He stood up for his people and never gave up, even during tough times. His strong will and love for freedom made him a hero. Today, his courage continues to inspire many Filipinos to stand proud and value their independence.
